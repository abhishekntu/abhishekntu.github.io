I am working as a PhD Student in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University. My research is supervised by Assoc. Prof. Douglas L. Maskell and Assoc. Prof. Suhaib A. Fahmy. I am associated with ARCH Research Group at CHiPES, Centre for High Performance Embedded Systems, since August 2012.

For my dissertation work, I worked as an intern at STMicroelectronics, India. Prior to that I was awarded MITACS Globalink Scholarship to pursue research work at Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, University of Alberta, Canada in May, 2011.
