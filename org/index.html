Overlay Reading Group (ORG) @ NTU

The members of Overlay Reading Group (ORG) meets weekly to present overlay related articles, discuss new ideas, and provide critical feedback on current research work.
This reading group is led by Assoc. Prof. Douglas L. Maskell and Asst. Prof. Suhaib A. Fahmy.
